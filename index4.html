What is Investing?

Investing is a great way to make generative passive income . Investing is a long term financial plan. There are many ways to invest. You can invest in stocks , bond , cryptocurrency ,and mutual funds.

Investing on the Stock Market

A few good platforms to start are Acorn , fidelity , ameritrade. When making an account , you will have two options - a brokerage account and a robo advisor account. A brokerage account is the easiest and least expensive way to buy stocks

  • Do you research , want well known companies that have long term growth ,like apple
  • Companies that have multiple market sectors , so they will not lose value if they fall in one market
